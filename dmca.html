DMCA Takedown Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

Toolsfast.online respects the intellectual property rights of others. We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and will respond to valid takedown notices.

Important: Toolsfast does not host, store, or cache any video content. We act as a technical tool that connects users to publicly available content on third-party platforms.

Filing a DMCA Notice

If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed, please send a notice to dmca@toolsfast.online including:

  1. Your name and contact information
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work
  3. URL(s) of the alleged infringing content
  4. A statement of good faith belief that the use is unauthorized
  5. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate and you are the copyright owner or authorized agent
  6. Your physical or electronic signature

Response Procedure

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  • Review the notice for completeness
  • Block access to the specific content if applicable
  • Notify the user who submitted the content (if applicable)
  • Respond within 48 business hours

Counter-Notice

If you believe content was wrongly removed, you may file a counter-notice with the same required information, stating that you believe the content does not infringe.

Contact
